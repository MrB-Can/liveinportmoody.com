**Treetops** sits on Parkside Drive in the heart of Heritage Mountain, one of Port Moody's most established and sought-after residential neighbourhoods. Built in the early 1990s, Treetops has the kind of long-term appeal that newer developments still haven't matched.

## Community and Setting

Located in Heritage Mountain, a quiet area surrounded by greenbelt, trails, parks, and outdoor recreation, yet still accessible to shopping, transit, and amenities in Port Moody Centre and Newport Village.

The complex is thoughtfully arranged in clusters, giving each home privacy and a natural backdrop. Walking the grounds, it feels more like a forested retreat than a traditional strata development.

## Homes and Layouts

Treetops consists of approximately **172 duplex-style townhomes** spread across multiple clusters on Parkside Drive.

- Multi-level homes with vaulted ceilings and large windows
- Floor plans ranging from 3-bedroom main-floor layouts with private patios to spacious 3–5 bedroom homes with walk-out basements and double garages
- Many homes feel genuinely house-like in scale: this is the kind of townhouse where a growing family doesn't feel cramped

The variety within the complex means resale values vary meaningfully by unit: corner homes, end units, and those backing onto greenspace command the highest premiums.

## Amenities

This is where Treetops stands apart from most strata townhouse communities in Port Moody:

- Indoor heated pool
- Sauna and hot tub
- Fully equipped exercise room
- Clubhouse with kitchen, popular for gatherings
- Outdoor basketball court
- Maintained grounds and landscaping included in strata fees

The amenity package gives residents a resort-like experience without leaving the complex.

## Location

- Walking distance to Heritage Mountain Elementary; close to Eagle Mountain Middle and Heritage Woods Secondary
- Easy access to greenbelt trails directly from the complex
- 15–20 minute drive to Port Moody Centre SkyTrain, or bus connection nearby
- Newport Village, Suter Brook, and Rocky Point all accessible within 10 minutes

## Why Buyers Stay at Treetops

✅ Spacious layouts with versatile floor plans that feel like single-family homes
✅ Quiet, scenic setting in Heritage Mountain's greenbelt
✅ Exceptional community amenities: pool, sauna, gym, clubhouse
✅ Strong family appeal given school proximity and outdoor access
✅ Established complex with a long track record of community investment

The resale market at Treetops tends to be competitive when units come up. The combination of size, amenities, and neighbourhood quality keeps demand steady even in slower overall markets.
