**Discovery Ridge** is on Forest Park Way in the heart of Heritage Mountain — one of Port Moody's quieter townhouse communities, valued for its privacy, practical layouts, and a genuinely residential atmosphere that larger complexes often can't replicate.

## Location

Forest Park Way is tucked into a peaceful residential pocket surrounded by mature trees and greenspace.

- Immediate access to parks and forested walking trails
- Close to **Bert Flynn Park** — one of Heritage Mountain's most popular outdoor spaces
- Within the catchment for **Heritage Mountain Elementary**, **Eagle Mountain Middle**, and **Heritage Woods Secondary**
- Convenient access to Newport Village, Suter Brook Village, and Port Moody Centre

The location offers suburban calm while remaining reasonably connected to everyday amenities.

## The Community

Discovery Ridge is a **smaller townhouse complex** — which is deliberate for many buyers. A smaller strata means fewer neighbours, a quieter environment, and often a more cohesive community feel. The flip side is a smaller reserve fund contribution base, so strata financial health is particularly worth reviewing.

Homes typically feature:

- Multi-level layouts with generous square footage
- Practical, family-friendly floor plans
- Private patios or outdoor spaces
- Attached garages and ample storage

The scale of the complex is better suited to buyers who prefer established, low-density living over newer high-density developments.

## Lifestyle

Discovery Ridge appeals to buyers who've specifically chosen Heritage Mountain for its residential character. You're close to the schools, the trails, and the parks — and the complex itself doesn't have the density or noise of some of the larger communities nearby.

It also tends to be more affordable per square foot than Treetops (which has the amenity premium) while offering similar neighbourhood access.

## What to Know Before Buying

As with other Heritage Mountain complexes from this era, building age and strata reserve fund status are the key due diligence items. Smaller complexes require careful review of the depreciation report — a major capital expense in a complex with fewer units means a proportionally larger hit to each owner.

## Why Buyers Choose Discovery Ridge

✅ Quiet, private complex with a genuine community feel
✅ Practical family-friendly layouts
✅ Surrounded by nature, parks, and walking trails
✅ Strong school catchment
✅ More affordable entry into Heritage Mountain compared to amenity-heavy complexes

For buyers who want Heritage Mountain access without the strata fee premium of Treetops or the scale of Quest, Discovery Ridge consistently comes up as a practical choice.
