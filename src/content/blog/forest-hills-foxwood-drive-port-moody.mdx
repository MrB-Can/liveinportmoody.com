**Forest Hills** sits on Foxwood Drive in Heritage Mountain — one of Port Moody's original established neighbourhoods, known for its mature trees, quiet streets, and proximity to some of the best trail systems in the Tri-Cities.

## Location

- Steps from **Bert Flynn Park** and its hiking trails — one of Heritage Mountain's most-used outdoor spaces
- Within the catchment for **Heritage Mountain Elementary**, **Eagle Mountain Middle**, and **Heritage Woods Secondary**
- Quick access to Newport Village, Suter Brook Village, and Rocky Point Park
- Sasamat Lake and Buntzen Lake are 15–20 minutes away — a meaningful quality-of-life factor for outdoor-focused buyers

## The Community

Forest Hills was built in the early **1990s** by Aragon Properties. The complex has **78 townhome units** with a classic, low-rise townhouse layout.

What makes Forest Hills distinctive is how well the units relate to the landscape. Many homes have **large windows framing greenbelt views**, and the complex backs onto forested space in several sections. If you're buying for the outdoor lifestyle Port Moody is known for, this is one of the complexes where you genuinely feel it from inside the home.

## Home Features

- **2-storey layouts** with generous living spaces and sizable bedrooms
- Private patios or fenced backyards — many with southern exposure
- Cozy gas fireplaces in living areas
- Practical kitchens and dining rooms that open to outdoor spaces
- **Underground parking and storage lockers**
- Clubhouse and playground on-site

Layouts suit a range of buyers: families needing multiple bedrooms, downsizers who want outdoor space without yard maintenance, and first-time buyers stepping up from a condo.

## What to Know Before Buying

At 30+ years old, Forest Hills is one of the older complexes in Heritage Mountain. The Aragon build quality from that era was generally solid, but buyers should review the depreciation report carefully. Strata reserves, envelope condition, and any upcoming capital work are the key questions.

The tradeoff for the age is price — Forest Hills typically comes in lower per square foot than newer complexes in the neighbourhood, which makes it one of the more accessible entry points into Heritage Mountain.

## Why Buyers Like Forest Hills

✅ Immediate trail access — Bert Flynn Park is genuinely steps away
✅ Private outdoor spaces, many with greenbelt backing
✅ Quiet, well-established complex with strong community character
✅ Good school catchments for families
✅ More affordable entry into Heritage Mountain than newer builds
