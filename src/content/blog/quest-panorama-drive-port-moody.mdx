**Quest** is on Panorama Drive in Heritage Woods, one of the larger townhouse complexes in Port Moody and one that often attracts family buyers because of its space, natural surroundings, and school-access questions worth verifying by exact address.

## Location

- Walking distance to **Aspenwood Elementary**, a significant draw for families with young children
- Close to **Heritage Woods Secondary**. Verify current school assignment and program details with SD43
- Surrounded by forested greenspace and walking trails
- Convenient access to Newport Village, Suter Brook, and Port Moody's waterfront
- Bus access to Port Moody Centre SkyTrain; Heritage Woods is one of the more transit-accessible Heritage Mountain sub-areas

## The Community

Quest was built in the **mid-2000s** and consists of approximately **130 townhome units**, one of the larger complexes in Heritage Woods. The West Coast architectural style uses natural materials that blend well with the forested surroundings.

Larger complexes generally have more robust strata finances simply because the contribution base is bigger. It's worth verifying, but Quest tends to have a healthy contingency reserve relative to smaller complexes nearby.

## Homes

- Typically **three-storey layouts** with attached garages
- Open-concept main living areas suited to entertaining and family life
- Modern kitchens (by early-2000s standards) with generous counter space and storage
- Large primary bedrooms; flexible rooms for home offices, playrooms, or guests
- Balconies or patios, many with treed outlooks
- Multiple outdoor spaces across the three levels

The square footage is genuine. These feel like houses, not condos with stairs.

## What to Know Before Buying

Quest is approximately 18–20 years old. The mid-2000s construction era in BC was generally well-built (post-leaky condo crisis), but depreciation report review, envelope condition, and strata financials are still standard due diligence.

The proximity to Aspenwood Elementary can matter to some buyers, but school assignment and value impact should be verified against the exact address, current SD43 information, and comparable sales context.
