**Viewpoint** is on Ravine Drive in Heritage Mountain — a street that earns its name by being genuinely tucked away from Port Moody's busier corridors. It's one of the quieter complexes in the neighbourhood, and that's a deliberate choice for many buyers who land here.

## Location

Ravine Drive sits off the main Heritage Mountain loop, meaning through-traffic is minimal. It has a residential-pocket feel that's rare to find this close to Port Moody's amenities.

- Close to **Heritage Mountain Elementary**, **Eagle Mountain Middle**, and **Heritage Woods Secondary**
- Easy access to Newport Village, Suter Brook Village, and Rocky Point Park
- Popular outdoor destinations — Sasamat Lake, Buntzen Lake — within 15–20 minutes
- Bus access to Port Moody Centre SkyTrain nearby

## The Community

Viewpoint was built in the **early 1990s** and consists of approximately **65 townhome units**. It's a mature complex with well-established landscaping and grounds that have had decades to develop.

Many units **back onto greenbelt or forest**, which is a material quality-of-life difference. If your patio faces trees rather than another unit's parking, the feel of the space changes entirely.

## Homes

- Typically **three-level layouts** with generous square footage
- Private patios or decks — many with forested or greenbelt views
- Attached garages
- Large windows bringing natural light and forest views into living areas
- Cozy gas fireplaces
- Multiple bedrooms and bathrooms suited to families or work-from-home needs

The three-storey layout means bedrooms are well-separated from living spaces — practical for families and anyone who needs a home office that actually functions.

## What to Know Before Buying

Like Forest Hills and Discovery Ridge nearby, Viewpoint is 30+ years old. The early-1990s construction era in Port Moody was generally solid, but envelope (siding, windows, roof), mechanical, and reserve fund questions are standard due diligence for any complex of this age.

The greenbelt-facing units command meaningful premiums over interior-facing ones. If you're comparing resale prices within the complex, that's the primary driver of price variation.

## Why Buyers Choose Viewpoint

✅ Quiet, private location — genuinely away from through traffic
✅ Greenbelt and forest backing on many units
✅ Spacious three-level layouts suited to families
✅ Strong school catchment proximity
✅ Established complex with mature landscaping

For buyers prioritizing peace and privacy over walkability or urban convenience, Viewpoint consistently delivers. It suits people who chose Port Moody specifically for the nature access and neighbourhood feel — not those commuting by SkyTrain every day.
