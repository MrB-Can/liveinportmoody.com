# Next Neighbourhood Batch Readiness Plan

**Sprint:** 20C  
**Project:** LiveInPortMoody.com  
**Scope:** Documentation-only readiness planning for Suter Brook, Newport Village, Klahanie, College Park, and Mountain Meadows.

This plan prepares the next batch after Heritage Woods, Moody Centre, and Glenayre. It does not create routes, change sitemap behavior, add IDX, add listing cards, add sold data, or publish any page.

## Batch Strategy

The next batch should not treat every target area as the same kind of guide.

- **College Park** and **Mountain Meadows** are closer to standard neighbourhood-guide candidates because they already have improved draft content, cautious school framing, FAQ, buyer/seller notes, and fallback listing config.
- **Suter Brook**, **Newport Village**, and **Klahanie** should be considered lifestyle, village, condo-area, or micro-area guides unless research confirms they should be handled as full neighbourhood pages.
- No building or complex should be attached to an area page until the relationship is verified from existing site data and building-specific research.
- All school language must remain address-specific and SD43-verification-first.
- All listing modules should use fallback CTA behavior until filtered MLS Reciprocity URLs are tested and approved.

## College Park

**Current readiness estimate:** 75%

**Recommended format:** Standard neighbourhood guide.

College Park is already an improved draft. It is the strongest next candidate if school, parks, trails, transit, and photo questions are resolved without creating overconfident claims.

**Key buyer appeal**

- Quieter west-side Port Moody residential setting.
- Access toward Barnet Highway, Burnaby, and SFU for some daily routines.
- Established housing mix, including detached homes, townhouses, and some strata options to verify.
- Potential fit for buyers comparing quieter Port Moody areas with hillside considerations.

**Key seller positioning**

- Position around quiet residential feel, west-side access, property-specific strengths, and verified recreation proximity.
- Emphasize lot usability, parking, storage, updates, exposure, views if present, and outdoor space only where supported by the property.
- Avoid overstating school assignment, walkability, views, or future redevelopment.

**School verification needs**

- Verify sample College Park addresses through SD43 before preview promotion.
- Keep Seaview Community School, Heritage Woods Secondary, and Port Moody Secondary as research prompts only until exact-address checks are complete.
- Add or confirm middle-school handling if the page will mention elementary, middle, and secondary categories separately.
- Retain language such as “some homes may be associated with,” “school assignment varies by exact address,” and “verify with SD43.”

**Parks/trails verification needs**

- Verify Westhill-area recreation references through City of Port Moody sources.
- Verify Seaview-area park references and any listed amenities.
- Verify Alfred Howe Greenway wording before publication.
- Do not claim specific park amenities, pool availability, off-leash rules, or trail status without official-source support.

**Transit/access verification needs**

- Verify west-side access framing toward Barnet Highway, Burnaby, and SFU.
- Avoid exact drive-time or transit-time claims.
- Check whether transit access varies materially enough to require stronger address-specific wording.

**Related buildings/complexes to research**

- No verified College Park related buildings or complexes are currently attached in `src/data/neighbourhoodGuides.ts`.
- Research only after confirming building location, address, guide status, and whether it belongs in College Park rather than a nearby area.
- Do not use broad “College Park townhomes” references as a live relationship until a real complex record exists.

**Photo needs**

- Residential streetscape photos that accurately reflect College Park.
- Park/recreation photos only after official park references are confirmed.
- Avoid using Moody Centre, Newport, Suter Brook, or waterfront imagery as substitutes.

**Listing module status**

- Current draft uses fallback listing mode with active-listings-only MLS Reciprocity disclosure.
- Keep fallback CTA only.
- Do not add listing cards, sold data, copied photos, IDX runtime, or a filtered MLS link until URL filtering is verified.

**Blockers before preview route**

- SD43 exact-address verification for school prompts.
- Official-source verification for Westhill, Seaview-area parks, and Alfred Howe Greenway references.
- Photo or explicit placeholder strategy.
- Final QA for school wording, parks/trails source framing, listing safety, and layout.

**Recommended priority:** 1

College Park should be first in the next batch if verification confirms the current cautious draft can stand without major rewrite.

## Suter Brook

**Current readiness estimate:** 35%

**Recommended format:** Lifestyle / village / condo micro-area, not necessarily a broad neighbourhood.

Suter Brook currently reads as a placeholder in the neighbourhood guide data. It is likely valuable, but the page should be framed around walkable village-style condo living rather than forced into a broad residential-neighbourhood template.

**Key buyer appeal**

- Daily convenience, shops, services, restaurants, and central Port Moody access.
- Condo and townhouse comparison context.
- Transit-oriented lifestyle for buyers prioritizing walkability and less car-dependent routines.
- Useful comparison point against Newport Village, Moody Centre, and Klahanie.

**Key seller positioning**

- Position around convenience, walkability, building-specific strengths, exposure, parking, storage, amenities, and strata health.
- For condo sellers, the strongest copy should likely be building-specific rather than broad-area copy.
- Avoid making generalized claims about every building or every address in the area.

**School verification needs**

- Determine whether schools should be a small verification note rather than a major page section.
- Verify any school prompt through SD43 by exact address before naming schools.
- Avoid catchment certainty because Suter Brook is likely more building/address-dependent than broad-neighbourhood-dependent.

**Parks/trails verification needs**

- Verify which official City parks, paths, and public spaces are appropriate to mention.
- Distinguish Suter Brook amenities from nearby Moody Centre, Newport Village, Rocky Point, and inlet-side access.
- Do not copy trail data or imply trail status.

**Transit/access verification needs**

- Verify official transit references and station proximity wording.
- Avoid exact walking-time, commute-time, or door-to-platform claims.
- Distinguish general transit-oriented positioning from exact building-level access.

**Related buildings/complexes to research**

Candidate records already exist in `src/data/buildings.ts`, but each relationship and boundary should be verified before use:

- Aria 1
- Aria 2
- Libra
- Suter Brook buildings
- The Grande, only after resolving its “Suter Brook / Port Moody Centre context” boundary wording

**Photo needs**

- Village streetscape and retail/public-realm photos.
- Building exterior photos only with building-specific permission/appropriateness and accurate labels.
- Avoid implying a specific building relationship from generic village imagery.

**Listing module status**

- Current guide placeholder uses `mode: "none"`.
- Recommended preview state: fallback listing CTA with active-listings-only MLS Reciprocity disclosure.
- Filtered MLS links should wait until provider URL behavior is tested for micro-area, building, and address search.

**Blockers before preview route**

- Decide final page type: micro-area/lifestyle guide versus neighbourhood guide.
- Replace placeholder intro and “coming soon” copy.
- Add buyer/seller notes, school verification framing, parks/trails official-source framing, transit/access notes, trade-offs, FAQ, and fallback listing config.
- Verify candidate building relationships and boundary wording.
- Create photo/placeholder plan.

**Recommended priority:** 3

Suter Brook has strong user value, but it needs more structural decisions than College Park or Mountain Meadows.

## Shared Verification Checklist

Before any of these areas becomes a preview route:

- Confirm page type: neighbourhood, lifestyle/micro-area, village, condo area, or nearby/related area.
- Remove placeholder or “coming soon” language.
- Use SD43 exact-address verification framing for schools.
- Verify parks, trails, fields, amenities, public spaces, and recreation claims through official sources where possible.
- Avoid exact commute-time, walk-time, school-catchment, value-growth, or amenity certainty claims.
- Add fallback listing CTA with active-listings-only MLS Reciprocity disclosure.
- Do not add fake listing cards, sold data, copied listing photos, IDX runtime, or custom listing detail pages.
- Verify related buildings/complexes from actual data and addresses before linking or displaying them.
- Add a photo or explicit placeholder strategy that does not borrow imagery from the wrong area.
- Run route smoke tests, noindex checks, sitemap checks, and layout QA only after routes are intentionally created in a later sprint.
