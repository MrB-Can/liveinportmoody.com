## 3. Testimonials / Reviews

Please provide only approved review material:
- Public review link.
- Reviewer name as it may appear publicly.
- Exact review excerpt approved for website use.
- Source platform, such as Google, RankMyAgent, or RateMyAgent.
- Approval to publish the excerpt on LiveInPortMoody.com.

## 4. Verified Accolades / Badges

For each accolade, badge, award, ranking, or recognition, provide:
- Exact name.
- Year.
- Issuing organization.
- Public source link or proof.
- Approved logo/badge file, if it can be used.
- Any usage rules or required wording.

If the recognition is not public or approved for marketing use, do not include it yet.

## 5. Sold Property Content

If sold property content will be used later, provide:
- Property address or approved public display version.
- What may be shown publicly.
- Photos approved for reuse, if any.
- Written permission or compliance confirmation.
- Whether price, date, or story details may be used.

Do not send sold prices or sold-property examples for public display unless sharing is permitted in the applicable professional context.

## 6. Featured Businesses

For each suggested business, provide:
- Business name.
- Website or official public profile.
- Category.
- Port Moody area served.
- Why it matters to residents or people considering a move.
- Contact person, if permission is needed.
- Any approved photo or logo usage.

Businesses will be reviewed one at a time. No business will be listed as endorsed unless that wording is specifically approved.

## 7. Events

For each event, provide:
- Event name.
- Organizer.
- Date and location.
- Public event link or registration page.
- Whether it is recurring or one-time.
- Why it is relevant to Port Moody residents.
- Any source notes or change risks.

Events should not be published without a public source or organizer confirmation.

## 8. Presales / New Builds

If presale or new-build content will be used, provide:
- Project name.
- Developer or sales team.
- Official project URL.
- Current public status.
- What may be said publicly about availability, pricing, deposits, assignments, GST, completion timing, and incentives.
- Source for each claim.

Do not publish availability, pricing, incentives, or completion timing unless the information is current and approved.
