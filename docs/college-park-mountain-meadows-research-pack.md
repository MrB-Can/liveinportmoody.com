## Shared Verification Notes

- School references must be verified by exact address with SD43 before being used in buyer guidance or property-specific advice.
- Do not make absolute catchment claims. Use phrases like "often checked," "nearby," "commonly associated," or "verify by exact address."
- Park, trail, recreation, pool, dog walking, sports field, and amenity claims should be checked against City of Port Moody pages immediately before publication.
- Trail descriptions should stay general. Do not copy trail data, trail status, route stats, GPS tracks, or difficulty ratings from Trailforks or other third-party trail databases.
- No listing inventory, sold data, pricing claims, days-on-market claims, school ranking claims, or unsupported future-development claims should be added from this pack.

### Buyer Fit

College Park may fit buyers who:

- Want a quieter residential feel while staying in Port Moody.
- Value west-side access toward Burnaby/SFU or Barnet Highway.
- Are comfortable comparing hillside streets, slope, driveway grade, and car dependence.
- Want detached homes, townhomes, or older/established strata options, subject to exact inventory.
- Prioritize recreation access over central-village walkability.

### Seller Positioning

Potential seller themes:

- Position around quiet residential setting, west-side access, and recreation proximity.
- Emphasize verified property-specific strengths: views, lot usability, exposure, parking, storage, updates, suite potential if legal/verified, and strata condition where relevant.
- Avoid overstating school certainty, walkability, views, or future redevelopment.
- For hillside homes, pre-empt buyer questions about drainage, retaining walls, driveway grade, access, roof/envelope condition, and winter practicality.

### Housing Types

- Verify the exact micro-area before publishing a housing profile.

### Key Trade-Offs

- Quieter setting versus less central village-style walkability.
- Hillside/topography benefits versus driveway, stairs, drainage, and winter access questions.
- West-side commuter access versus distance from some east-side Port Moody amenities.
- Established housing stock can bring character and lot appeal, but also renovation, strata, envelope, or maintenance due diligence.
- Transit convenience may vary significantly by address.
