## Do NOT Invent

This list explicitly prevents fake content. DO NOT:

- [ ] Invent testimonials or fake client names
- [ ] Invent awards or badges
- [ ] Invent business listings or local business profiles
- [ ] Invent event dates or event details
- [ ] Invent sold properties or fake client stories
- [ ] Claim real estate qualifications you don't have
- [ ] Present legal/tax/financial advice as fact

All testimonials, reviews, accolades, businesses, and events must be:
1. Real
2. Owner-approved
3. Properly sourced or attributed
4. Compliant with privacy and professional standards
