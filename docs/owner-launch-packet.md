# Owner Launch Packet

LiveInPortMoody.com is ready as a controlled-launch real estate and local-life resource. It is built to help people compare Port Moody neighbourhoods, buildings, complexes, listings, buyer and seller paths, and local context without publishing unverified claims.

## What the site is now

- A Port Moody real estate decision guide for buyers, sellers, relocators, and homeowners.
- A local authority site with neighbourhood, building, complex, presale, Local Life, Featured Businesses, Events, Meet Us, Raving Fans, and resource pages.
- A lead-capture site with real contact and guide-request forms.
- A compliance-conscious preview site that avoids fake listings, sold data displays, fake testimonials, fake awards, fake business listings, fake events, and unsupported claims.

## What is behind coming-soon

The public site is still protected by coming-soon mode. Visitors without preview access see the coming-soon page. Preview users can review the full site with the unlock flow.

Preview neighbourhood, building, and complex pages remain marked so search engines should not index them until they are intentionally made public.
