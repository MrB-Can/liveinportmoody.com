# 30-Day Post-Launch Content Roadmap

This roadmap focuses on local authority, lead generation, and verified content after LiveInPortMoody.com launches.

## Week 1: Launch Follow-Up and Trust Basics

- Confirm live forms are creating the right GoHighLevel contacts.
- Review the first real inquiries and note which pages generated them.
- Add approved social profile URLs.
- Collect approved team photo or decide on a no-photo launch period.
- Ask Leilani to identify the first 3 testimonials/reviews that can be verified and approved.
- Confirm MLS Reciprocity URL status and listings fallback wording.

## Week 2: Local Authority Foundations

- Pick 3 priority neighbourhoods for deeper content updates.
- Add owner-approved local photos where available.
- Start one Featured Business intake using the business workflow below.
- Start one Events intake using the event workflow below.
- Draft one buyer-focused article from a real common question.
- Draft one seller-focused article from a real common question.

## Week 3: Lead-Magnet and Proof Improvements

- Review Buyer Guide and Seller Guide request pages based on early form activity.
- Decide whether each guide remains a request/waitlist page or becomes a finished downloadable asset.
- Add approved review links or excerpts to Raving Fans if available.
- Add verified accolades or leave Accolades as a verified-content holding page.
- Create one short YouTube/social topic from a guide page.
- Review recent-sales request language after any real inquiries.

## Week 4: Depth and Repeatable Content System

- Publish or improve one neighbourhood guide from the priority list.
- Improve one building or complex guide with verified facts and owned/approved media.
- Publish the first approved Featured Business profile if ready.
- Publish the first verified Events item or keep the page as an event suggestion path.
- Review analytics and form submissions for top entry pages.
- Plan the next 30 days based on actual traffic and inquiry themes.

## Sold Story Intake

Use only if sold-property sharing is permitted and approved.

- Confirm what can be shared publicly.
- Confirm whether price, date, address, photos, and client story details may be used.
- Get written approval for any client story.
- Avoid private client details.
- Frame the story as education, not a guaranteed result.
- If approval is unclear, do not publish the sold story.

## Featured Business Workflow

- Collect business name, website, area served, category, and why it matters locally.
- Confirm public source details.
- Ask for permission before using non-public photos, logos, quotes, or story details.
- Review one business at a time.
- Avoid endorsement language unless explicitly approved.
- Connect the feature back to neighbourhood/lifestyle context.

## Local Event Workflow

- Collect event name, organizer, public source link, date, location, and registration details.
- Confirm whether the event is one-time or recurring.
- Check source freshness before publishing.
- Avoid stale dates or unverified hosts.
- Include change-risk language when useful.
- Remove or update event content after it becomes outdated.

## Review / Accolade Collection Workflow

- Collect public review links or approved excerpts.
- Confirm exact approved wording.
- Do not publish review counts or star ratings unless source-backed.
- Collect accolade name, year, issuing organization, and source proof.
- Confirm badge/logo usage rights.
- Keep unverified recognition off the site.

## Neighbourhood Content Depth Plan

Prioritize updates that support real buyer decisions:

1. Suter Brook / Newport Village / Klahanie condo lifestyle comparison.
2. College Park and Glenayre quiet residential comparison.
3. Heritage Woods and Heritage Mountain family / trail context.
4. Ioco / North Shore, Anmore, and Belcarra nearby-community framing.
5. Building and complex links inside each relevant neighbourhood page.

For every guide, verify school, trail, boundary, strata, and listing-specific claims before relying on them.

## Social / YouTube Funnel Ideas

- "Which Port Moody area fits your lifestyle?"
- "Condo vs townhouse vs detached in Port Moody."
- "What to verify before buying a Port Moody strata property."
- "How to request recent sales context safely."
- "Moving to Port Moody: what surprises buyers."
- "How Featured Businesses shape local-life decisions."
- "One neighbourhood, one trade-off" short video series.
- "Ask Leilani" buyer/seller question clips.

Every video or post should link back to one useful page: Contact, Buyer Guide, Seller Guide, Neighbourhoods, Local Life, Listings, or Request Recent Sales.
