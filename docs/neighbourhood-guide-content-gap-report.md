## Quality Checklist Before Publishing Each Guide

- ☐ Intro complete and descriptive (2-3 sentences)
- ☐ bestFor list has 3-4 relevant buyer personas
- ☐ housingTypes list accurate and specific
- ☐ tradeOffs list has 2-4 honest considerations
- ☐ Schools: Either complete with SD43 verification OR documented as "verify with SD43"
- ☐ Parks & Trails: At least 1-2 entries per neighbourhood (or documented as "limited")
- ☐ transitAndAccess: Specific and honest (not overclaimed)
- ☐ walkabilityNotes: Honest assessment (high/moderate/low)
- ☐ Buyer Notes: 3-5 specific considerations (strata, grading, commute, etc.)
- ☐ Seller Notes: 3-5 marketing angles aligned with bestFor personas
- ☐ FAQ: 3-5 neighbourhood-specific questions (not generic)
- ☐ SEO: Title (50-60 chars) and description (150-160 chars) filled
- ☐ Photos: Status = "complete" OR "pending_approval" (not "not_started")
- ☐ Listings: Mode configured (gvr_area_search or fallback for Port Moody; "none" for placeholders)
- ☐ Related Complexes: Wired if major complexes exist; empty acceptable otherwise
- ☐ Latitude/Longitude: Accurate coordinates present
- ☐ No school catchment certainties (all use "may fall within", "verify with SD43")
- ☐ No exact commute times (all use "varies by location", "verify for address")
- ☐ No price predictions or appreciation claims
- ☐ Build compiles without errors (npm run build)
- ☐ Lint passes without warnings (npm run lint)
